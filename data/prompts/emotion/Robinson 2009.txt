Contrasted emotion families (Robinson review, 2009).

Families:
- Interest/Curiosity/Enthusiasm
- Alarm/Panic
- Attraction/Desire/Admiration
- Aversion/Disgust/Revulsion
- Surprise/Amusement
- Indifference/Habituation/Boredom
- Hope/Excitement
- Fear/Anxiety/Dread
- Gratitude/Thankfulness
- Anger/Rage
- Joy/Elation/Triumph
- Sorrow/Grief
- Patience
- Frustration/Restlessness
- Contentment
- Discontentment/Disappointment/Depression
- Humility/Modesty/Confidence
- Pride/Arrogance
- Charity/Sympathy
- Avarice/Greed/Envy/Jealousy
- Love
- Hate

Context:
- Classify by emotional function and object relation.

Examples:
1) "I'm on edge and expecting something bad." -> Fear/Anxiety/Dread
2) "I appreciate what you did for me." -> Gratitude/Thankfulness
3) "I want to stay near them all day." -> Attraction/Desire/Admiration
