Descartes primary passions framework (1649).

Categories:
- Wonder
- Love
- Hate
- Desire
- Joy
- Sadness

Context:
- Speaker focus on novelty, attraction, aversion, wanting, pleasure, or pain.
- Immediate appraisal of events and intentions.

Examples:
1) "I can't stop staring at this strange new phenomenon; I've never seen anything like it." -> Wonder
2) "I feel drawn to them and want to stay close." -> Love
3) "Everything feels heavy and I just want to cry." -> Sadness
