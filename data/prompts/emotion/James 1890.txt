William James basic-emotion set (1890).

Categories:
- Fear
- Grief
- Love
- Rage

Context:
- Fear: threat, alarm, avoidance.
- Grief: loss, sorrow, mourning.
- Love: attachment, affection, care.
- Rage: intense anger and hostile action tendency.

Examples:
1) "My chest tightened when I heard footsteps behind me at night." -> Fear
2) "Since she passed, I feel hollow every morning." -> Grief
3) "He cut in line again and I wanted to yell and slam the door." -> Rage
