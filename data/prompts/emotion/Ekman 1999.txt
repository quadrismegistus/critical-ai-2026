Ekman expanded basic-emotion set (1999).

Categories:
- Anger
- Disgust
- Fear
- Happiness
- Sadness
- Surprise
- Amusement
- Contempt
- Contentment
- Embarrassment
- Excitement
- Guilt
- Pride in achievement
- Relief
- Satisfaction
- Sensory pleasure
- Shame

Context:
- Prioritize the behaviorally central emotion in mixed affect states.

Examples:
1) "I finally submitted the thesis and can breathe again." -> Relief
2) "I can't believe I said that in front of everyone." -> Embarrassment
3) "I nailed the exam and feel proud of my effort." -> Pride in achievement
