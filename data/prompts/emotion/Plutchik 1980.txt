Plutchik wheel of emotions (1980).

Primary categories:
- Joy
- Trust
- Fear
- Surprise
- Sadness
- Disgust
- Anger
- Anticipation

Context:
- Emotion dyads may appear in text; map to the nearest primary category.

Examples:
1) "I'm looking forward to the launch tomorrow." -> Anticipation
2) "I feel betrayed and bitter." -> Anger
3) "I feel close and safe with her." -> Trust
