Shaver et al. hierarchical prototype model (1987).

Primary categories:
- Love
- Joy
- Surprise
- Anger
- Sadness
- Fear

Context:
- Nuanced descriptors map to the nearest primary family.

Examples:
1) "I miss them deeply and feel abandoned." -> Sadness
2) "I'm curious and delighted by this unexpected gift." -> Surprise
3) "I feel tenderness and affection for him." -> Love
