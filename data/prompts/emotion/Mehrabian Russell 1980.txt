PAD model (Mehrabian and Russell, 1980).

Dimensions:
- Pleasure
- Arousal
- Dominance

Category profiles:
- Joyful (P+, A+, D+)
- Relaxed (P+, A-, D+)
- Content (P+, A-, D-)
- Excited (P+, A+, D-)
- Angry (P-, A+, D+)
- Anxious (P-, A+, D-)
- Sad (P-, A-, D-)
- Bored (P-, A-, D+)

Examples:
1) "I feel in control, upbeat, and ready to act." -> Joyful
2) "I feel threatened and powerless." -> Anxious
3) "I feel low-energy and down." -> Sad
