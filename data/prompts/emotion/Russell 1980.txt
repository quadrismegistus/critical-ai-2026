Russell circumplex model (1980).

Dimensions:
- Valence
- Arousal

Canonical categories:
- Excited (high arousal, positive valence)
- Happy (moderate arousal, positive valence)
- Calm (low arousal, positive valence)
- Relaxed (very low arousal, positive valence)
- Bored (low arousal, negative valence)
- Sad (low-moderate arousal, negative valence)
- Distressed (moderate-high arousal, negative valence)
- Angry (high arousal, negative valence)

Examples:
1) "I'm buzzing with energy and optimism." -> Excited
2) "Everything feels pointless and slow." -> Bored
3) "I'm furious and shaking." -> Angry
