Lazarus and Lazarus appraisal-based categories (1996).

Categories:
- Aesthetic experience
- Anger
- Anxiety
- Compassion
- Depression
- Envy
- Fright
- Gratitude
- Guilt
- Happiness
- Hope
- Jealousy
- Love
- Pride
- Relief
- Sadness
- Shame

Context:
- Emphasize appraisal patterns: harm/loss, threat, challenge, moral self-evaluation, relational meaning.

Examples:
1) "I feel thankful they stayed late to help me." -> Gratitude
2) "I'm worried I'll fail and ruin everything." -> Anxiety
3) "I want what he has and resent his success." -> Envy
