Context:
- Fine-grained continuous emotion space represented with discrete category anchors.

Examples:
1) "That cathedral was breathtaking and left me speechless." -> Awe
2) "I miss those childhood summers so much." -> Nostalgia
3) "I felt secondhand pain watching her get humiliated." -> Empathic pain
