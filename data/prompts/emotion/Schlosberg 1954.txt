Schlosberg three-dimensional model (1954).

Dimensions:
- Pleasantness-Unpleasantness
- Attention-Rejection
- Activation level (low-high)

Category profiles:
- Pleasant Attention High Activation
- Pleasant Attention Low Activation
- Pleasant Rejection High Activation
- Pleasant Rejection Low Activation
- Unpleasant Attention High Activation
- Unpleasant Attention Low Activation
- Unpleasant Rejection High Activation
- Unpleasant Rejection Low Activation

Examples:
1) "That new idea is fascinating and energizing." -> Pleasant Attention High Activation
2) "I hate this and want it away from me right now." -> Unpleasant Rejection High Activation
3) "I feel bored and disengaged by this topic." -> Unpleasant Rejection Low Activation
