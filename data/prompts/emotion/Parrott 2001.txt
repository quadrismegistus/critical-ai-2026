Parrott tree-structured emotions (2001).

Primary groups:
- Love
- Joy
- Surprise
- Anger
- Sadness
- Fear

Context:
- Secondary and tertiary terms map to one primary group.

Examples:
1) "I'm resentful and full of spite." -> Anger
2) "I'm hopeful and optimistic about tomorrow." -> Joy
3) "I'm panicking and feel dread." -> Fear
