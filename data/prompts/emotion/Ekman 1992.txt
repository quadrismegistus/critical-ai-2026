Ekman basic emotions (1992).

Categories:
- Anger
- Disgust
- Fear
- Happiness
- Sadness
- Surprise

Context:
- Emphasize core affect signal over subtle social nuance.
- Mixed signals map to the dominant emotion.

Examples:
1) "I jumped when the plate suddenly shattered behind me." -> Surprise
2) "The smell made my stomach turn." -> Disgust
3) "I can't stop smiling; this is the best day ever." -> Happiness
