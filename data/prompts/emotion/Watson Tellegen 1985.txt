Positive Activation-Negative Activation model (Watson and Tellegen, 1985).

Dimensions:
- Positive affect
- Negative affect

Category profiles:
- High Positive Affect
- Low Positive Affect
- High Negative Affect
- Low Negative Affect
- Mixed High (both high)
- Flat Low (both low)

Context:
- Positive and negative affect are partially independent axes.

Examples:
1) "I'm energized, enthusiastic, and engaged." -> High Positive Affect
2) "I'm tense, upset, and irritable." -> High Negative Affect
3) "I'm emotionally muted and detached." -> Flat Low
